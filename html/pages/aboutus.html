Urban Douxe Cafe

Welcome to our first class cafe, where a rich aroma of a freshly brewed coffee mingles
with the sweeetness of
our handcrafted cupcakes and delicious icecream.

We believe that great coffee, ice cream, and cupcakes can brighten anyone’s day,
and we are here to make that magic happen daily. Our baristas, bakers, and ice cream makers are passionate
artisans, blending quality ingredients, creativity, and a
sprinkle of fun into every cup, scoop, and bite.

Here at the Doux Cafe,
We are all about supporting sustainable practices—from sourcing coffee
beans and dairy to choosing eco-friendly packaging. We aim to create a space where
everyone feels welcome to savor a moment with loved ones, unwind, or be inspired.
Whether you are stopping by for your morning espresso, a mid-day ice cream pick-me-up, or
a late-night cupcake, we are excited to be a part of your day!

Thank you for joining us on this delicious journey, we are thrilled to be your go-to spot for life's little indulgences.



Vision:To become a cherished local hotspot, reowned for our top-notch products
and unforgettable customer service that leaves everyone smiling.


Mission:Delight the community with bold flavors in coffee,
ice cream, and cupcakes, creating a lively, inviting hub for
connection, celebration, and unforgettable treats.
